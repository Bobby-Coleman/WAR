WAR!

What is it good for? Learning web development!

War is a game where a deck of cards is shuffled and equally divided into two decks, one for the player and one for the dealer. Then, the top card from each deck is flipped and their values compared. Winner takes both cards. If there are no cards left in your pile, you lose! *WAR-ning, this game requires no skill*
